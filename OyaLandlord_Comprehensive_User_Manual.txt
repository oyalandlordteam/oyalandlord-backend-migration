             OYALANDLORD: COMPREHENSIVE USER MANUAL & FEATURE GUIDE
=============================================================================
*(Keep this file in the project folder. It will be updated as new features are added)*

Welcome to the comprehensive, human-readable user documentation for the OyaLandlord platform. This document explains exactly how the app works from a user's perspective, step by step, button by button.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE HOME PAGE (Welcome Screen)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
When a user first opens the app (localhost:3001), they land on the Home Page.

WHAT THEY SEE:
- A large welcoming hero image with the text "Rent Without Agent Fees".
- Search Bar: Two main inputs -> Location (e.g., Ikeja, Yaba) and Property Type (Flat, House, Room, etc.).
- "How It Works" Section: Three columns explaining the platform for Tenants, Landlords, and Solicitors.
- "Featured Properties": A carousel showing 4 highly-rated or promoted homes.
- "Why Choose OyaLandlord": A grid of 6 reasons explaining the benefits (Zero Agent Fees, Direct Chat, etc.).
- "Questions & Answers": An accordion (collapsible list) where clicking a question reveals the answer.
- Top Navbar: A sticky bar at the top with "Home", "Browse", "Login", and "Register" buttons.

BUTTONS & ACTIONS:
- [Search Button]: Clicking this on the hero section takes the user directly to the "Browse/Tenant Dashboard" with their filters applied.
- [Dark Mode Toggle]: A small moon/sun icon in the top right that switches the app between light and dark visual themes.
- [Login Button]: Opens the Login page.
- [Register Button]: Opens the Registration page.


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. REGISTRATION AND LOGIN (Authentication)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
In order to use the core features, users must create an account.

REGISTER PROCESS:
- The user clicks [Register] at the top right of the screen.
- They fill out: Full Name, Email Address, and Password.
- Role Selection: The user MUST click one of three buttons to define who they are:
  - "I am a Tenant" (Looking to rent)
  - "I am a Landlord" (Looking to list properties)
  - "I am a Solicitor" (Looking to provide legal verification services)
- Upon clicking [Create Account], the system saves their profile and logs them in immediately.

LOGIN PROCESS:
- The user clicks [Login].
- They enter their Email and Password.
- Clicking [Sign In] verifies their details and redirects them to their specific Dashboard based on the role they chose during registration.


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. THE TENANT EXPERIENCE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
If a user registers as a Tenant, they get access to a specific dashboard tailored for finding and renting properties.

A. TENANT DASHBOARD (Browse Properties)
- What it is: A massive grid of all available properties on the platform.
- Filters: On the left side or top, they can tweak the Price Range, Bedrooms, and Amenities (like Power Supply or Security) and the list updates instantly.
- Property Cards: Each property shows a picture, price, location, and bed/bath count. 
- Action: Clicking anywhere on a Property Card opens the "Property Details" page.

B. PROPERTY DETAILS PAGE
- What it is: A full deep-dive into a specific house. Shows a gallery of images, a detailed description, and a breakdown of the price (Rent + Caution Fee, etc.).
- [Save/Heart Button]: Adds the property to their "Favorites" list for later.
- [Book Inspection Button]: Clicking this opens a pop-up calendar and time-picker. The tenant selects when they want to visit the property and clicks [Submit Request]. This sends a notification to the Landlord.
- [Make Offer Button]: Allows the tenant to negotiate. They type in how much they are willing to pay and hit send.

C. INSPECTIONS TAB (My Bookings)
- What it is: A list of all the visits the tenant has requested.
- Statuses: Shows whether the Landlord has "Approved", "Rejected", or is still "Pending" the visit. 

D. RENTALS TAB
- What it is: Once the tenant pays and signs a lease, the active agreement lives here.
- Actions: They can click [Download Agreement] to save a PDF of their lease, or see how many months are left before rent is due.


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. THE LANDLORD EXPERIENCE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Landlords have a command center designed for managing their property portfolios and handling tenant requests.

A. LANDLORD DASHBOARD
- Overview Stats: Three big cards at the top showing "Total Properties", "Active Rentals", and "Total Views" (how many people clicked on their houses).
- My Properties List: A table showing every property they own.
- [Add New Property Button]: Opens the creation form.
- Action Buttons (Next to each property):
   - [Edit]: Change the price, photos, or description.
   - [Toggle Visibility]: A switch that hides the property from the public if it gets rented out.
   - [Delete]: Removes the property from the system entirely.

B. ADD/EDIT PROPERTY FORM
- What it is: A long form where the landlord builds their listing.
- Inputs required: Title, Description, Location, Price, Number of Beds/Baths, and uploading Images.
- Amenities Selector: Checkboxes to tag the property with things like "Borehole Water", "24/7 Security", "Prepaid Meter".
- Price Breakdown: Landlords can add specific line items (e.g., Rent = 1M, Caution = 100k, Service Charge = 50k).
- Clicking [Publish Property] immediately makes it live on the Tenant Dashboard.

C. INSPECTION REQUESTS PANEL
- What it is: A chronological list of tenants who want to visit the landlord's properties.
- Actions: The landlord reviews the requested Date/Time and clicks either the green [Approve] button or the red [Reject] button.


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. THE SOLICITOR EXPERIENCE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Solicitors serve as legal verifiers on the platform.

A. SOLICITOR DASHBOARD
- What it is: A queue of properties and agreements waiting for legal sign-off.
- The solicitor reviews the documents uploaded by the landlord.
- Action: They click [Add Legal Note] to type comments about the property's legal standing (e.g., "C of O verified, safe to rent").


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. THE ADMIN EXPERIENCE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Admins (the platform owners) have ultimate control over the system.

A. ADMIN OVERVIEW
- What it is: A master dashboard tracking the health of the entire business (Total Users, Total Revenue across the app, Total Properties).

B. USER MANAGEMENT (Verify / Suspend)
- What it is: A table of every system user.
- Actions:
  - [Verify Button]: Crucial for Landlords and Solicitors. Admins review their real-world documents and click Verify to give them a "trusted badge" on the platform.
  - [Suspend Button]: Revokes a user's access if they violate rules.

C. CONTENT MANAGEMENT (CMS)
- What it is: This is where admins update the text that appears on the public website without needing a developer.
- Actions: They can edit the "Frequently Asked Questions", the "About Us" page, and the "Terms and Conditions". Clicking [Save] instantly updates the live website for everyone.


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
SYSTEM WIDE FEATURES
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Notifications: A bell icon at the top right of every dashboard shows a red dot when something happens. (E.g., "Your inspection was approved!" or "You received a new bid!").
- Dynamic Logouts: Clicking User Profile -> Logout reliably clears the user's data and sends them back to the Home Page.
